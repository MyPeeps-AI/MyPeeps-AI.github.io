What is MyPeeps.Ai?

MyPeeps.Ai is a platform that allows you to create and interact in natural ways with a set of AI characters, we call these companions Peeps. We offer an intuitive and simple web UI to subscribe to pre-made Peeps, or to create your own in a few simple steps and talk to them within minutes.

Our goal is to maximize immersion and take away the technical worries of combining and implementing the latest technical features. We are NOT developing our own LLMs or generative AI, nor do we offer our own cloud hosting. Our strategy is to be flexible and combine leading technologies into a convenient package.

Peeps may create chat responses with visual input through the OpenAi API, using Google Cloud Text-to-Speech, ElevenLabs or any other TTS to create the Peeps voice. Peeps will adapt live Image & Video-generation on-demand, with the overall goal of getting the same online capabilities as humans.
